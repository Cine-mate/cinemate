About Us


Brand Values
Community Connection

We believe in the power of experiencing cinemas together. Our website allows movie lovers to connect and share their experiences allowing them to build meaningful connections.

Inclusivity

Everyone deserves to enjoy the cinema experience without feeling lonely. Our website is made for everyone, allowing for inclusivity, where people can feel welcomed and connected.

Empowerment Through Technology

This Website enables our customers to plan outings, form groups, and expand their social circles, empowering them in terms of their social life and planned experiences.

Enhancing Enjoyment

Going to the movies alone can be lonely. So, we provide you with a platform where you can make connections and friends with similar interests, we are enhancing your cinema experience.

Our Team

Cinemate is created by 4 students who have a passion for cinemas and bringing people together. At Cinemate we believe in connecting people through cinematic interests and allowing for empowerment in socialization. Our platform offers connectivity to those in need of social outings to the cinema. It's a place where you can vote on movies, and be linked up with people with similar interests. You can match and chat, and see offers and deals that suit your perfect day out.

Our Brand Persona

Name: Anna Hill

Age: 21

Education: 2nd year college student

Work: Part time waitress in a restaurant

Hobbies: reading, Writing and watching movies

Interests: Reading, Seeing or hearing stories. Preferably with someone to talk to about these stories.

Other: lives at home, has a small group of friends that do not always share the same interests.

Problem: she does not have many friends that share this interest.

Goal: she wants to meet people to share her interests with.

How she feels: She feels lonely and bored. She feels Lonely because she will not go to the cinema alone and she has no one to go with. She will not go alone because she dislikes the feeling of being alone when doing something she normally does with friends. She feels bored because she will not enjoy her free time by going to the cinema, because she has no one to go with.

How she acts: she is looking online for a way to meet like-minded people to enjoy her hobby with. She is open to try friends like Bumble to make friends. She looks up similar apps that she sees advertisements from on social media.
