About Us


Brand Values
Community Connection

We believe in the power of experiencing cinemas together. Our website allows movie lovers to connect and share their experiences allowing them to build meaningful connections.

Inclusivity

Everyone deserves to enjoy the cinema experience without feeling lonely. Our website is made for everyone, allowing for inclusivity, where people can feel welcomed and connected.

Empowerment Through Technology

The website enables our customers to plan outings, form groups, and expand their social circles through the magic of movies, empowering them in terms of their social life and planned experiences.

Enhancing Enjoyment

Going to the movies alone can be lonely. So, we provide you with a platform where you can make connections and friends with similar interests, we are enhancing your cinema experience.

Our Team

Cinemate is created by 4 students who have a passion for cinemas and bringing people together. At Cinemate we believe in connecting people through cinematic interests and allowing for empowerment in socialization. Our platform offers connectivity to those in need of social outings to the cinema. It's a place where you can vote on movies, and be linked up with people with similar interests. You can match and chat, and see offers and deals that suit your perfect day out.
